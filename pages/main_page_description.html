Although the system is similar to Google Analytics, it features unique functionality for working with traders:

  • Finteza is the only service which can provide an end-to-end out-of-the-box analytics. Find out the real client attraction cost, optimize marketing channels and boost ROI and LTV.

    The service is tightly integrated with MetaTrader 4/5 platforms: all actions of a potential client are recorded starting from the first site visit, while further statistics are collected directly from the platform. You can check the budget spent to attract and encourage the client, as well as your resulting profits.


  • With the built-in traffic attribution, third-party services are no longer needed and thus you can save on your marketing budget. Finteza registers all sources, supports different attribution models and shows conversion rates from your website and platform.

    15 reports are available for comprehensive analysis. In addition to conversion sources, you can analyze audience characteristics (geography, devices and platform, among others) as well as trader behavior (pages, events in the platform and on the site, bounce rate, detailed user logs and other relevant data).


  • Bot detector will analyze the quality of your purchased advertising traffic. It will show the amount of fake users and will help in determining reasons for low conversion rates.

    To compile traffic quality reports, the system checks blacklists to detect spammers, bot farms and DDoS attackers, as well as analyzes each user behavior in your app or site.


  • Detailed yet easy-to-use sales funnels can show lead and client loss points. You will be able to optimize weak stages and increase the efficiency of your marketing spendings.

    The system registers website and platform user behavior, groups users by attraction channels and generates comprehensive reports. Based on detected conversion loss points, you will be able to optimize your site and boost profits.


  • Bonus: built-in advertising engine with the retargeting function. Encourage your traders to make a deposit, to open a new account or to join a promotion. These processes are fast and easy with the integrated ad system. Using the retargeting option, you can display the right message at the right time and thus maximize the efficiency of trader engagement campaigns.
